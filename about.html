Welcome to ScratchStats.cf!

ScratchStats.cf is a free tool that uses the Scratch API to create a list of more than 150 statistics based on Scratch. These statistics range from user-based statistics (such as how much followers a user has), to global statistics (such as who has the most followers).



Example Statistics:

  • User-based Statistics:
    • # of Followers!
    • Average # of Gained Followers per day!
    • Most Viewed Project!
    • Most Followed Follower!
    • and more than 100 more!

  • Global Statistics:
    • Most Followed Scratcher!
    • Most Viewed Project!
    • Chosen FPC Curators!
    • Most used location!
    • and more than 50 more!
